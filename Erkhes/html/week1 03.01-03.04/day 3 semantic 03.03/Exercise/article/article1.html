The Advantages of VR Simulation

Virtual Reality (VR) Training immerses learners in a digital environment where they encounter real-life scenarios that test their knowledge without facing real-world risk. Regardless of what you use VR training for, there are overarching advantages and disadvantages to its use, including: Pros: Creates a Safe Learning Environment Exciting and Engaging Realistic Technical Skills Practice Collects Key Training Metrics Cons: Physical Side Effects Technology Developments and Updates High Cost
